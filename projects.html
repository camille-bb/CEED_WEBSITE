2023 - Shirley Bot üçí

This semester project involved designing and building an autonomous robot capable of creating Shirley Temple mocktails. The robot was programmed to precisely measure and dispense grenadine, lemon-lime soda, and garnish with cherries, automating the entire process. Our team worked on integrating sensors for accuracy, a user-friendly interface for drink selection, and mechanical systems for efficient ingredient handling. We aimed to optimize for consistency, speed, and presentation, blending engineering skills with a fun, hands-on application in beverage automation. The project highlighted key aspects of robotics, fluid mechanics, and user-centered design.
