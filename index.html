WE ARE CEED.


Who We Are

The mission of CEEDC is to inspire the next generation of entertainment innovators through our three pillars:

Industry Education

Provides hands-on industry experience through semester-long projects in areas like themed entertainment and live event production. Students collaborate on innovative designs, build professional portfolios, and gain skills for careers in entertainment technology.


Community Projects

Engages in community projects by creating interactive installations and entertainment experiences for local organizations and events. These projects allow members to apply their skills while giving back to the community, fostering creativity and collaboration in meaningful ways.

Interdiciplinary Collaboration

Fosters interdisciplinary collaboration by bringing together students from various backgrounds. Members work in diverse teams to design and execute innovative projects, combining technical expertise and creative vision to explore the intersections of engineering and entertainment.


What We Do

Engineering

CEED applies diverse engineering skills to creative projects, such as building furniture go-karts and developing interactive robots. Members use mechanical design, electrical systems, software programming, and prototyping to bring imaginative concepts to life, blending technical expertise with artistic innovation.

Software

CEED's software team focuses on web design, creating visually engaging and user-friendly websites. Members develop skills in front-end and back-end development, UX/UI design, and interactive media, contributing to both club projects and professional-grade online experiences.

Production

The production team manages events and handles business operations, combining creativity with organizational skills. Members plan and execute projects, coordinate logistics, and engage with sponsors, gaining experience in event production, marketing, and leadership.
